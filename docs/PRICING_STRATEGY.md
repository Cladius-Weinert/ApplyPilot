# Pricing Strategy

ApplyPilot is positioned as a practical workflow product for remote jobseekers, AI data workers, freelancers, and application tracking users.

## Positioning

ApplyPilot helps users:

- Discover better remote and AI-work opportunities.
- Organize applications and follow-ups.
- Tailor resumes and cover letters more consistently.
- Build proof-of-work portfolio assets.
- Maintain a repeatable daily application workflow.

ApplyPilot does **not** guarantee interviews, job offers, hiring, clients, or income.

## Suggested monthly placeholders

These are placeholders until validated with real users:

- Free: `$0/mo`
- Starter: `$19/mo placeholder`
- Pro: `$49/mo placeholder`
- Agency: `$149/mo placeholder`

## MVP boundary

This repository currently ships pricing and subscription structure as product documentation only. No payment provider, Stripe checkout, webhook, card storage, or hosted subscription authorization is active in this MVP.

## Tier logic

Free should be useful enough to build trust, but limited to 5 job leads per day. Starter monetizes the daily workflow and scoring. Pro monetizes advanced AI-worker assets such as evaluator portfolios, prompt libraries, RWS/AOP workflows, freelance gig generation, and priority scoring for platforms such as Mercor, Outlier, TELUS, Mindrift, OneForma, RWS, and Prolific. Agency monetizes multi-profile/client workflows.

## Compliance and trust copy

Use plain language:

- "No guaranteed jobs or income."
- "ApplyPilot helps improve consistency and organization."
- "You stay responsible for reviewing applications and respecting job-board rules."
- "We do not store payment card data directly."

Avoid claims such as:

- "Guaranteed job in 14 days"
- "Earn $X per day"
- "Bypass LinkedIn restrictions"
- "Auto-apply to every job"
